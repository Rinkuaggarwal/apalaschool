APALA SCHOOL OF EDUCATION

Functioning under the aegis of The Delhi Public Society, New Delhi, Delhi Public School, Hisar, situated on NH-9 between two historical cities, Hisar and Hansi, is one of the most prestigious C.B.S.E. Affiliated Schools of the region. Endowed with State Of The Art Infrastructure, Lush Green Premises and World Class Facilities, the school has become a rendezvous of more than 1200 aspiring learners from the vicinity. Irrespective of their color, caste, creed and religious denomination, here they receive the fruits of education and prepare themselves for the University of Life. Sown fourteen years ago as a tiny sapling by its Philanthropist ProVice Chairman, Sh. Munshi Ram Ji, the school has now flourished into a full-fledged blossoming tree catering to the educational needs of the impressionistic minds in the optimum possible manner. Sprawled over more than 17 acres of land at a prime location, the school is striving with an altruistic spirit and implementing the ideal educational policies and procedures while rendering qualitative and authentic services to its all stake holders.


Our well qualified, erudite and highly committed teaching faculty is religiously devoted towards performing a wide array of duties and responsibilities to justify the task entrusted upon them. With a perfect blend of theoretical and practical knowledge imparted in sophisticatedly furnished smart class rooms, they make studies a pleasant and interesting experience for the knowledge seekers.
