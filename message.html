Principal Message

Dear Parents & Students

It's my pleasure to welcome you to Apala School Of Education, Apala ensures a secure, emotionally balanced and healthy environment to all our students. One of the biggest goal for the year is to have all round development of your child and ensure his/her academic excellence. Apala Planner is designed to help the child in organizing his daily, weekly and long term assignments. It will also develop his/her organizational skills. This planner will also act as a communicative tool between the parents and teachers. We request you to notify the changes in the record of the student such as an address, telephone number, mobile number etc. We welcome your participation and support during the academic year. Please take a moment and visit our school website. you may feel free to contact me with any queries or concerns.
